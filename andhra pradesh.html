ANDHRAPRADHESH

Andhra Pradesh (English: /ˌɑːndrə prəˈdɛʃ/,[13] Telugu: [ãːndʱrɐ prɐdeːʃ] (listen) abbr. AP) is a state in the south-eastern coastal region of India.[14] It is the seventh-largest state by area covering an area of 162,975 km2 (62,925 sq mi)[4] and tenth-most-populous state, with 49,386,799 inhabitants.[15][16] It is bordered by Telangana to the north-west, Chhattisgarh to the north, Odisha to the north-east, Tamil Nadu to the south, Karnataka to the west and the Bay of Bengal to the east.[17] It has the second longest coastline in India after Gujarat, of about 974 km (605 mi).[18] Andhra State was the first state to be formed on a linguistic basis in India on 1 October 1953
